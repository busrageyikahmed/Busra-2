Beyond the Storefront - Istanbul

This project centers around the digital transformation of a traditional brick-and-mortar fashion boutique. Faced with the evolving landscape of retail, the boutique, which has been exclusively offline for an extended period, is exploring the introduction of online services. Our research aims to identify key opportunities and challenges associated with this transition, ultimately informing strategic decisions for the boutique's successful entry into the online realm.

Problem

Our client has been exclusively operating through physical retail channels. However, with the changing landscape of retail and consumer preferences, the owner recognises the need to adapt and is confronted with the challenge of introducing online services. The client is seeking solutions that allow for a successful online presence while maintaining the boutique's original charm and authenticity.

Solution

Comprehensive research will delve into the preferences, expectations, and behaviors of the boutique's target audience. This includes understanding how customers currently perceive the brand, their online shopping habits, and the specific elements of the in-store experience that contribute to the boutique's unique charm. Additionally, market research will identify emerging trends and successful strategies within the online retail landscape, providing insights into effective digital integration while maintaining authenticity. By employing a customer-centric research methodology, the boutique can gather valuable data to inform strategic decisions, ensuring that the online expansion not only meets the client's objectives but also resonates authentically with the boutique's established brand identity.
